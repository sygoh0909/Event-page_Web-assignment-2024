Event Volunteer

Event Volunteer Sign-Up

We are looking for volunteers for the following roles:

  • Stage Setup: Assist in setting up the stage and arranging equipment.
  • Guest Registration: Help with checking in guests and managing registrations.
  • Merchandise Booth: Handle sales and inquiries at the merchandise booth.
  • Security: Ensure the safety of guests and manage crowd control.
  • Cleanup: Assist in cleaning up after the event and ensuring the venue is left tidy.
  • Sound Crew: Set up and operate sound equipment, ensure audio quality throughout the event, and troubleshoot any audio issues.
  • Ushering and Seating: Guide guests to their seats, assist with any seating-related inquiries, and ensure the seating area is orderly.
  • Lighting Crew: Set up and operate lighting equipment, ensure proper lighting throughout the event, and troubleshoot any lighting issues.
  • First Aid/Medical Assistance: Provide basic medical assistance and coordinate with professional medical staff in case of emergencies.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age requirement for volunteers?

The minimum age requirement is 18 years old.

What should I wear as a volunteer?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and shoes, as you'll be on your feet for extended periods.
