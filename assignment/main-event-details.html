Main Event Details

Bobbin' with Blob The Flog: A Musical Journey

Dive into a whimsical world where enchanting melodies
and adventurous tales come alive!


Date: August 26, 2024

Time: 6:30 PM - 10:00 PM

Location: Sunway Lagoon

Ticket Price: RM50 for General, RM100 for VIP

About Us

Welcome to "Bobbin' with Blob The Flog: A Musical Journey"! We are thrilled to introduce ourselves as the creators and organizers of this unique musical event. Our team, fondly known as Blob The Flog, is a passionate group of music enthusiasts dedicated to bringing imaginative and vibrant musical experiences to life.

Why We Do This

We are dedicated on creating unforgettable live music experiences. Our name reflects our commitment to pushing the boundaries of musical performance and connecting with audiences in a unique and memorable way. We believe in the power of music to unite, inspire, and transform, and we are committed to delivering an event that resonates with audiences of all ages.

Event Theme

"Bobbin' with Blob The Flog: A Musical Journey" - Dive into a whimsical world where enchanting melodies and adventurous tales come alive! Our theme reflects our dedication to crafting a magical experience that blends music, storytelling, and visual artistry, taking you on a journey unlike any other. Join us as we explore captivating realms and let the music guide you through an unforgettable adventure.
